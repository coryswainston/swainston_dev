Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE) Prevention Standards

Patio · Effective May 7, 2026

Swainston Software Development LLC is committed to the safety and protection of children. We have zero tolerance for child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE) content or behavior on our platform.

Our Standards

The following are strictly prohibited on Patio:

  • Content that sexually exploits or endangers children, including child sexual abuse material (CSAM)
  • Grooming behavior or any conduct intended to facilitate the sexual exploitation of minors
  • Sharing, distributing, or soliciting any content that depicts or promotes the sexual abuse or exploitation of children
  • Using the app to contact minors for inappropriate or exploitative purposes

Prevention Measures

We take the following measures to prevent CSAE on our platform:

  • We review reported content and take swift action to remove any material that violates these standards
  • We cooperate fully with law enforcement agencies investigating CSAE
  • We report instances of apparent child exploitation to the National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC) and other relevant authorities as required by law
  • We permanently ban users found to be in violation of these standards

Reporting

If you encounter any content or behavior on Patio that you believe constitutes child sexual abuse or exploitation, please report it immediately:

  • Email us at cory@swainston.dev
  • Report to the National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC)
  • Contact your local law enforcement

We take all reports seriously and will investigate promptly.

Enforcement

Violations of these standards will result in immediate action, including but not limited to:

  • Immediate removal of offending content
  • Permanent suspension of the offending account
  • Reporting to law enforcement and NCMEC

Contact Us

If you have any questions about these standards, please contact us at cory@swainston.dev.
